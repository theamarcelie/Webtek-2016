About the project

During a designer study trip to Milano in March 2016, Peder saw a bench with leather made from salmon. Even for a Scandinavian product designer, this was a new experience. He returned to Norway, where salmon skin is an endless resource, and started experimenting. The result? Durable, beautiful and sustainable products

Peder

Peder is wrecklesly interested in everything that is truly beatiful and at the same time love technology. To suplement this, he an Industrial Design Student at NTNU. At his spare time Peder competes in triathlon. This describes him well as he never focuses on the short goal, the success at the end the most important. Like in his project of creating the new leather for the fashion industry.
