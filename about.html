About the project

During a designer study trip to Milano in March 2016, Peder saw a bench with leather made from salmon. Even for a Scandinavian product designer, this was a new experience. He returned to Norway, where salmon skin is an endless resource, and started experimenting. The result? Durable, beautiful and sustainable products

Peder

Peder is incredibly passionate about everything that is truly beatiful and at the same time he loves technology. To supplement this, he is an Industrial Design Student at NTNU. Peder competes in triathelon, which describes him well, as he is never focused on the short goal, the success at the end is the most important.
