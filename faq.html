During the tanning process the material becomes extremely tough and durable. It will withstand damages both in the form of scratches and tears. It is important to keep it out of direct sunlight for long periods though, because like other skin products it can be susceptible to bleaching and cracking caused by sunlight.

Salmon skin is naturally durable against staining. We recommend using a cloth with water, or if needed water with some light detergent. This is an efficient method against most stains. You should not use any heavy detergents to clean the products, as this may cause discoloring and other types of damage to the product. If water and light detergent does not remove the stain you should rather contact a professional cleaning agency.

Products made of fish skin will last you a lifetime with the right care. We recommend occasionally using leather conditioner to moisturise the skin, which will keep it from drying out and cracking.

We are not currently producing any products, and are still at the research fase of the project. But we hope to start making designs as soon as possible, and get our great ideas out to the market!
