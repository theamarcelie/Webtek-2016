Frequently Asked Questions

How durable is the material?

During the tanning process the material becomes extremely tough and durable. It will withstand damages both in the form of scratches and tears. It’s important to keep it out of direct sunlight for long periods though, because like other skin products it can be susceptible to bleaching and cracking caused by sunlight.

How can i clean products made with fish skin?

Salmon skin is naturally durable against staining. We recommend using a cloth with water, or if needed water with some light detergent. This is an efficient method against most stains. You should not use any heavy detergents to clean the products, as this may cause discoloring and other types of damage to the product. If water and light detergent doesn’t remove the stain you should rather contact a professional cleaning agency.

How do i preserve products made with fish skin?

Products made of fish skin will last you a lifetime with the right care. We recommend occasionally using leather conditioner to moisturise the skin, which will keep it from drying out and cracking.
